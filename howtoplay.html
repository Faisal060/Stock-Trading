How to Play

The goal is to increase your portfolio value by buying and selling stocks at the right time. Start with $10,000 and make trades based on stock prices.

  1. Buying Stocks: Click "Buy" to purchase shares of a stock if you think its price will go up.
  2. Selling Stocks: Click "Sell" to sell shares if you think the price will fall or to take profits.
  3. Winning: The player with the highest portfolio value wins!
